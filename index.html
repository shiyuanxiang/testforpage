HEALTH INSURANCE AGREEMENT

Policy Number: HG-2024-847392

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

This Health Insurance Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between HealthGuard Insurance Company ("Insurer") and the policyholder ("Insured"). This Agreement outlines the terms and conditions of your health insurance coverage.

1. COVERAGE DETAILS

1.1 Medical Services: This policy covers hospital stays, emergency room visits, physician consultations, diagnostic tests, and preventive care services as defined in the Schedule of Benefits.

1.2 Prescription Drugs: Coverage includes generic and brand-name prescription medications listed in the approved formulary, subject to applicable copayments and deductibles.

1.3 Preventive Care: Annual physical examinations, immunizations, and health screenings are covered at 100% with no cost-sharing when using in-network providers.

2. PREMIUMS AND PAYMENTS

2.1 Monthly Premium: The Insured agrees to pay the monthly premium of $450.00 on or before the first day of each month. Failure to pay within the 30-day grace period may result in policy termination.

2.2 Deductible: An annual deductible of $1,500 applies to covered services, except preventive care. The deductible resets on January 1st of each calendar year.

2.3 Out-of-Pocket Maximum: The annual out-of-pocket maximum for in-network services is $6,000 per individual. Once reached, the Insurer covers 100% of eligible expenses for the remainder of the year.

3. LIMITATIONS AND EXCLUSIONS

3.1 Pre-existing Conditions: Coverage for pre-existing conditions begins immediately upon policy effective date, in compliance with applicable healthcare regulations.

3.2 Experimental Treatments: Services deemed experimental, investigational, or not medically necessary are not covered under this policy.

3.4 Cosmetic Procedures: Cosmetic or elective procedures not medically necessary are excluded from coverage, except in cases of reconstructive surgery following illness or injury.

4. CLAIMS PROCESS

4.1 Filing Claims: Claims must be submitted within 180 days of service date. Claims can be filed online through the member portal or by mail using the standard claim form.

4.2 Processing Time: The Insurer will process and respond to submitted claims within 30 business days of receipt of complete documentation.

5. TERMINATION

5.1 Voluntary Termination: The Insured may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing 30 days written notice to the Insurer.

5.2 Termination by Insurer: The Insurer may terminate this Agreement for non-payment of premiums, fraud, or material misrepresentation on the application.

6. GENERAL PROVISIONS

6.1 Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the state in which the policy is issued.

6.2 Amendments: This Agreement may only be amended by written agreement signed by both parties. The Insurer reserves the right to modify coverage terms and premiums upon policy renewal.

6.3 Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements, representations, or understandings.

By signing below, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to all terms and conditions outlined in this Health Insurance Agreement.
